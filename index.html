Joshua Selvidge

Joshua Selvidge is a cybersecurity entrepreneur with a deep passion for helping SMBs and startups secure their digital assets. As the CTO of PurpleSec, he leverages his extensive experience in software development, network security, and compliance to drive innovative security solutions. Joshua is dedicated to improving the security posture of organizations, making complex cybersecurity concepts accessible, and fostering a culture of continuous learning and improvement in the field.
